About CarbonTracker

CarbonTracker is a tool that enables users to calculate, track, and reduce their carbon footprint by analyzing factors such as transportation habits, energy consumption, and lifestyle choices. By providing insights and suggestions, CarbonTracker empowers individuals to make informed decisions and take actions to minimize their environmental impact.
